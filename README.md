# 📊 Power BI Data Analytics Portfolio

Welcome to my Power BI analytics portfolio! This repository contains three distinct Power BI projects that demonstrate my ability to solve business problems through data visualization, data modeling, and advanced DAX calculations.

## 🚀 Projects Overview

### 1. 🎓 NCAA Financial Analysis
**Domain:** Sports Management & Finance
**Goal:** To analyze the financial health and profitability of major NCAA conferences over a 10 year period (2010–2020).


* **The Problem:** Stakeholders needed a consolidated view of revenues versus expenses across different athletic conferences to identify the most profitable organizations.
* **The Solution:** Developed a comparative financial dashboard to track net profit and expense trends.
* **Key Features:**
    * **Conference Comparison:** visualized the SEC as the top performer with **$17bn** in total revenue.
    * **Financial Trends:** Area charts tracking the growth of **$86.6B** in total revenue against **$83.0B** in expenses.
    * **Profitability Analysis:** Calculated net profit metrics for major conferences like B10, PAC12, and ACC.

### 2. 🛒 Maven Market Sales Report
**Domain:** Retail & Sales
**Goal:** To track regional sales performance, product profitability, and return rates for a multi-national retail chain.

* **The Problem:** The business required visibility into store performance across North America (USA, Canada, Mexico) to optimize brand inventory.
* **The Solution:** Created an interactive report enabling drill downs by country and product brand.
* **Key Features:**
    * **Geospatial Analysis:** Interactive map visualization tracking transactions across North American store locations.
    * **Brand Performance:** Matrix table analyzing distinct brands (e.g., *Hermanos*, *Ebony*), identifying high-margin products (approx. 60% margins).
    * **KPI Monitoring:** Gauge charts tracking monthly revenue against targets and monitoring return rates (avg 0.99%).

### 3. 📈 SuperStore Profit Margin Analysis
**Domain:** E-Commerce & Operations
**Goal:** To identify regional profit discrepancies and analyze profit margin trends over time.

* **The Problem:** High sales volume was not always translating to high profit, requiring a deep dive into regional efficiency.
* **The Solution:** A focused dashboard highlighting profit margin trends by region and time period.
* **Key Features:**
    * **Regional Benchmarking:** Identified the **West Region** as the most efficient (14.94% margin) compared to the Central Region (7.92%).
    * **Time Series Analysis:** Line charts tracking profit margin volatility from 2017 to 2020.
    * **High-Level KPIs:** Consolidated view of Total Sales (**$2.3M**) and Total Profit (**$286K**).
